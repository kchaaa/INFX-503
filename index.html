My True
Seattle Experience

Hi my name is Kevin and I'm a sophomore at University of Washington for the intended major of Informatics.
Last year, I took a class called CSE 131 (Science and Art of Digital Photography). Before taking this class, I never took an interest in photography and did not know about the beauty of Seattle besides U-District, Pike's Place, and the Space Needle. I knew Seattle on a shallow basis and felt like a tourist. Thanks to CSE 131, I was given the opportunity and an excuse to explore Seattle. I fell love in with the different neighborhoods of Seattle and know more about the city than I know about my hometown, San Francisco. If you really want to start exploring, go on google maps and look up the directions. I hope by telling you about my experience visiting Downtown Seattle, Ballard, Fremont, and Queen Anne, they will convince you to also explore and fall in love with Seattle.

Here is a quick description of each location:

Downtown: The Most Iconic Scene of Seattle
Ballard: The Nice and Quaint Beauty of Seattle
Fremont: The Food Scene of Seattle
Queen Anne: The Greater Area of Seattle
